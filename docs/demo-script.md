# Demo Script

Target length: 2 minutes.

Live demo: https://ai-fitness-coach-spread.butterbase.dev/

## 0:00-0:20 Problem

New members often leave before they ever feel confident using the gym. Staff cannot personally re-explain the floor, equipment, safety cues, and first-week plan for every new signup.

## 0:20-0:40 Product

AI Fitness Coach turns one gym profile and one member profile into a personalized five-video onboarding series. It uses the studio's actual equipment, class mix, and brand voice, then tailors the plan to the member's goal, experience level, and limitations.

## 0:40-1:35 Live Flow

Fastest version:

1. Open the live demo.
2. Click `Run instant demo`.
3. Show the five cards transitioning from pending to generating to ready.
4. Click `Start watching`.
5. Highlight the personalized title, member context, brand voice, coaching notes, and up-next strip.

Full local version:

1. Create a gym profile with `Demo fill`.
2. Open the owner dashboard.
3. Add a member with `Demo fill`.
4. Generate the onboarding plan.
5. Open the ready video player.

## 1:35-2:00 Close

The wedge is boutique gyms and fitness studios that want a premium first-week experience without adding staff hours. The next layer is member share links, retention analytics, and white-label delivery for studios.
